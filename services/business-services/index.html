How Cybersecurity Works with ATS Edmonton

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from malicious threats like hacking, malware, and ransomware. It involves multiple layers of defense designed to prevent, detect, and respond to security breaches before they can cause damage. ATS Edmonton employs advanced cybersecurity protocols that protect your business from every angle, leveraging cutting-edge technology and industry best practices.

Key Components of Cybersecurity at ATS Edmonton:

1. Network Security

We focus on securing the infrastructure of your business, including firewalls, intrusion detection systems (IDS), and secure VPNs to prevent unauthorized access and ensure secure communication within your organization.

2. Endpoint Security

Every device connected to your network—whether desktops, laptops, or mobile devices—presents a potential threat. ATS ensures that all endpoints are protected against malware, ransomware, and unauthorized access.

3. Data Protection

Our data protection services ensure that your sensitive business and customer data remains safe through advanced encryption techniques, data loss prevention (DLP), and regular backups. ATS helps your organization comply with regulations such as GDPR and CCPA, providing the highest level of security to protect your valuable data.

4. Identity & Access Management (IAM)

We offer IAM solutions that control who has access to your company’s systems and data. Through multi-factor authentication (MFA) and role-based access control (RBAC), ATS ensures that only authorized individuals can access critical systems, reducing the risk of internal and external breaches.

5. Incident Response

In the event of a cyberattack, ATS provides rapid incident response services to contain, mitigate, and recover from security breaches. Our expert team works quickly to reduce damage and get your business back to normal, while analyzing the attack to improve future defenses.

6. Risk Management

We perform comprehensive cybersecurity risk assessments to identify vulnerabilities and reduce your exposure to cyber threats. With ATS Edmonton’s expert risk management solutions, you’ll stay ahead of potential vulnerabilities and safeguard your business operations.

Cybersecurity Services Provided by Alberta Tech Solutions (ATS) Edmonton

At ATS Edmonton, we deliver a wide range of cybersecurity services to businesses across various industries. Our tailored solutions address your unique challenges and ensure the safety of your infrastructure, data, and digital assets.

1. Managed Security Services (MSSP)

24/7 Cybersecurity Monitoring: With ATS Edmonton as your Managed Security Service Provider, your systems are continuously monitored to detect and respond to threats in real-time. Our managed security solutions proactively prevent cyber threats, minimize vulnerabilities, and provide constant oversight of your network.

2. Penetration Testing (Pen Test)

Simulated Cyberattacks: We conduct comprehensive penetration tests to identify weak spots in your network. By simulating real-world attacks, we help you fortify your defenses before malicious hackers can exploit your vulnerabilities.

3. Threat Intelligence & Threat Hunting

Stay Ahead of Emerging Threats: With ATS cybersecurity services, we provide actionable threat intelligence, using real-time data and AI-powered analysis to stay ahead of evolving cyber threats. Our threat-hunting techniques actively search for hidden risks within your systems, reducing the chance of undetected breaches.

4. Data Loss Prevention (DLP) & Encryption Services

Protect Your Sensitive Data: We use state-of-the-art data encryption tools to protect your data at rest, in transit, and during processing. Our DLP solutions prevent unauthorized access and ensure that your business complies with data privacy regulations like GDPR and PCI-DSS.

5. Network Security & Firewall Management

Secure Your Infrastructure: ATS helps secure your network with next-generation firewalls, intrusion prevention systems (IPS), and secure Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) to keep your business safe from unauthorized access, cyberattacks, and malware.

6. Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)

Real-time Monitoring & Threat Detection: We use SIEM solutions to consolidate logs, monitor network activity, and detect security incidents in real time. This proactive approach helps identify and mitigate risks before they lead to significant breaches.

7. Cloud Security

Secure Your Cloud Environment: Whether you’re leveraging public cloud services like AWS, Microsoft Azure, or private cloud solutions, ATS Edmonton provides comprehensive cloud security solutions to ensure your cloud infrastructure remains secure and compliant.

8. Incident Response & Cyberattack Recovery

Minimize Damage from Cyberattacks: In the event of a security breach, our incident response team provides immediate containment, mitigation, and remediation. We also help with cyberattack recovery and root-cause analysis to prevent future incidents.

9. Compliance & Risk Management

Achieve Compliance with Industry Standards: ATS Edmonton helps businesses comply with industry regulations, such as HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI-DSS, and ISO 27001, ensuring that your business follows best practices and mitigates risks of non-compliance.

10. Employee Security Awareness Training

Empower Your Team: Human error is often the weakest link in cybersecurity. ATS Edmonton offers security awareness training to teach your employees how to recognize phishing attacks, avoid social engineering tactics, and follow best security practices to prevent breaches.
